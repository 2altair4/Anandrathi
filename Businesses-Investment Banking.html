Sponser Coverage

Comprehensive coverage and intense network among the global and domestic private equity community enables Anand Rathi to generate proprietary investment ideas for financial sponsors. We bank on our deep knowledge of PE firms to identify high-quality financial buyers for middle-market clients. We have developed a specialised practice for PE firms that want to invest in the India story, but do not have a presence in the country.





Cross Border M&A

Cross border transactions can be complex, but we can help you simplify them! Our cross border advisory team operates as an independent advisor for every domestic and foreign client that seeks to acquire technology, brand or market access. We function in close partnership with our clients and leverage our global network to find the right fit for them.





Equity Capital Markets

We are among the leading underwriters of public issues of mid-cap companies in India. Our expertise in providing innovative financing solutions to achieve complex corporate objectives has helped us create a niche in the mid-market segment.

At Anand Rathi, we offer extensive support to IPO clients through objective research coverage, equity sales and trading services. We rank among the top 3 in post-listing IPO performance in the mid-market segment in India.

We utilise our operational expertise and world-class institutional and retail distribution prowess to maximise value during primary and secondary offerings. Members of our team have collectively helped raise more than $20 billion in equity and debt capital over the course of their careers.
